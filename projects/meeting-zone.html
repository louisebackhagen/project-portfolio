Meeting Zone

2022–2023 User Research Interactive Prototyping User Testing Adobe XD CSS

Challenge

Board meetings in TeamEngine were spread across multiple tools and views. Users had to move between video conferencing solutions, calendar agendas, and document viewers to prepare for and participate in meetings. This fragmentation caused unnecessary context switching, reduced focus, and made collaboration during meetings less efficient.

The challenge was to design a single, cohesive meeting experience that could support complex board workflows—combining video, agenda, and active document collaboration—while remaining flexible, secure, and easy to use for users with different roles and technical confidence.

Process

Define: Synthesized research insights to define the core problem: board members needed a single, focused meeting space where video, agenda, and documents could coexist. Clear goals were set to reduce context switching, support active document collaboration, and provide a flexible interface for different roles and meeting phases.

Ideate: Explored multiple concepts for unifying meetings into one workspace. Challenged the assumption that video, agenda, and documents should live in separate views, which led to the idea of a tab-based meeting environment where users could seamlessly move between meeting activities.

Prototype: Created low- and mid-fidelity clickable prototypes to visualize user flows, layout behavior, and interactions. Prototypes focused on panel resizing, tab navigation, and document actions such as annotating, highlighting, and commenting during meetings.

Test: Conducted moderated lab usability testing with target users to evaluate the design in a controlled environment, validate interaction patterns, and identify usability issues and opportunities for improvement.

Iterate: Continuously refined the design based on user feedback and real-world usage. Given the size and duration of the project, iteration was ongoing rather than a single phase. This included close collaboration with developers during implementation, contributing to CSS styling to fine-tune layout behavior, spacing, and visual consistency, ensuring the final product matched the intended interaction design.

Delivery & Impact

Unified Meeting Workspace: Video conferencing, agenda, and documents combined in a single tab, eliminating the need to switch between multiple tools.

Live Agenda Tracking: Participants can follow the meeting agenda in real time, seeing who is speaking, how long each topic lasts, and what each point is about, increasing focus and efficiency.

Interactive Documents: Users can annotate, highlight, and comment on documents directly during meetings, supporting active collaboration.

Improved Efficiency: Reduced context switching and smoother workflows result in more productive meetings.

Secure Video Meetings: Built-in video conferencing ensures meetings are safe and private, with all content staying within TeamEngine.
